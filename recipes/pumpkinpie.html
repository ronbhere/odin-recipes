Mom's Pumpkin Pie

The one and only pumpkin pie! Becca's mom's recipe makes this traditional dessert the perfect ending to a Thanksgiving feast.
Garnish as desired. Refrigerate leftovers.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 tsp pumpkin pie spice
  • 3/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar (heaping)
  • 4 eggs
  • 29 ounce can 100% Libby's pure pumpkin
  • 1 package pillsbury pie crust (2cnt)
  • 1 can Carnation evaporated milk FF
  • 1 can Carnation evaporated milk 2%

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Take both of the pie crusts out of the box to warm 15-30 minutes. Preheat oven to 425 degrees.

  2. Step 2

    In a large bowl mix eggs, pumpkin, sugar and spices. Shake evaporated milk thoroughly and pour into the bowl. Mix with electric mixer until consistent.

  3. Step 3

    Press the pie crusts into the pie pans and trim any crust that hangs over the edge. Pour mixture into the pie pans evenly.

  4. Step 4

    Place both pies in the oven and bake at 425 degrees for 10 minutes(to help the crust brown). After 10 minutes, leave the oven door closed and
    reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees and bake for 75 minutes. Stick a butter knife in the center of pie, if the knife comes out clean, the pies are done.
